Treatment create:
    Standard crud menu. Create displays sub-menu (intake, pre-op, surg-prep, surgery, post-op and recovery).
    Intake creates instances (owner, animal, clinic) and outputs instances to pre-op.
    Pre-op selects from list of intake instances and enterd its data. Instance is then sent to swurg-prep.
    Surg-prep selects from list of pre-op instances and enters prep details. Instance is sent to surgery.
    Surgery selects from list of surg-prep instances and enters its data. Instance is sent to post-op.
    Post-op selects from a list of surgery instances ans fills in its data. Instance is sent to recovery.
    Recovery selects from a list of post-op instances and fills in its data. Instance is saved and closed.

Treatment list:
    Asks for clinic date and displays list of treatments for that clinic.

Treatment update:
    Asks for clinic date and treatments for that date are displayed. Update follows as normal.

Treatment delete:
        Asks for clinic date and treatments for that date are displayed. Delete follows as normal.
